    About

    There is a lot of information available on the internet. There are many social platforms connecting people. Our goal is to merge the two. Infosmos is a web platform that matches people for mutual enlightenment and knowledge sharing. It is inspired by the idea that the natural tendency of information is to flow from higher to lower concentration and that it is natural for people with similar interests to connect -- Therefore, we believe in "Osmosis of Information" or "Infosmos". To find a "buddy" who shares a common interest or find a "mentor" to guide you or for an expert who is looking for mentees, Infosmos is at your service!
